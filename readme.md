# EcoLisis-Website

The official website for **EcoPolis App** by EcoLisis - Sustainable Solutions. EcoPolis is a sustainability-focused app that bridges the gap between municipalities, businesses, and citizens, rewarding eco-friendly actions and promoting community engagement for a greener future.

## üå± Project Overview

EcoPolis App aims to:
- Encourage sustainable habits by rewarding users for reducing single-use plastics.
- Facilitate food donations from businesses to those in need.
- Help users find recycling points and green spots in their area.
- Support stray animal care by mapping feeding stations and adoption opportunities.
- Enable item exchange and donation within the community.

## üöÄ Features

- **Reward System:** Earn points for eco-friendly purchases and redeem them in future transactions.
- **Food Donation:** Businesses can donate surplus food to people in need.
- **News & Actions:** Stay updated on local municipality news and initiatives.
- **Stray Animal Support:** Find and support stray animals and feeding stations.
- **Recycling Map:** Locate nearby recycling points and green spots.
- **Exchange & Donate:** Exchange or donate items you no longer need.
- **Social Integration:** Connect with us on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/EcopolisApp), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/eco_lisis/), and [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/EcoPolis-App-102843069029400).
